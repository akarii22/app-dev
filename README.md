**Stranger Things** is a science fiction horror television series created by the Duffer Brothers.

## Plot Summary
In 1980s Indiana, a group of young friends witness supernatural forces and secret government exploits. As they search for answers, the children unravel a series of extraordinary mysteries.

### Main Characters
- **Eleven** 
- **Mike Wheeler**
- **Dustin Henderson**
- **Lucas Sinclair**
- **Will Byers**

## Why I Love This Series

I enjoy the mix of 80s nostalgia, suspenseful storytelling, and strong character development.
